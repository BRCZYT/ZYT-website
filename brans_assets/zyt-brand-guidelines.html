/ Statement

A brand system engineered for precision, presence, and global scale. This document defines the visual language of ZYT Global Industry — to be applied consistently across digital, print, environmental, and product touchpoints.

01 / Logo

The primary mark

Custom typographic logo with engineered chamfers on Z, Y, and T — never recreate, distort, or substitute.

03 / Spatial rules

Clearspace & minimum size

Maintain a margin equal to the cap-height of the T (= X) on all sides. Never reproduce below 24px digital or 12mm print.
