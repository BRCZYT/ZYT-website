\#\# LINKEDIN METRICS

Track weekly. Review monthly.

| Metric | Starting Baseline | Month 3 Target | Month 6 Target | Month 12 Target |  
|--------|------------------|----------------|----------------|-----------------|  
| Followers | 0 | 300 | 800 | 2,000 |  
| Impressions/week | — | 2,000 | 8,000 | 25,000 |  
| Engagement rate | — | 3%+ | 4%+ | 4%+ |  
| Comments/post | — | 3+ | 8+ | 15+ |  
| DMs/week | — | 2+ | 5+ | 10+ |  
| Profile views/week | — | 50 | 150 | 400 |

Engagement rate formula: (Likes \+ Comments \+ Shares) / Impressions × 100  
Acceptable: 2–3% | Good: 3–5% | Excellent: 5%+

\#\#\# Decision Triggers — LinkedIn  
If engagement rate \< 2% for 2 consecutive weeks:  
→ Review hook quality of last 5 posts. Change format (try list, try question, try bold statement).

If follower growth stalls for 3 weeks:  
→ Increase outbound connection requests (20/day, personalized). Engage on others' posts in your target audience.

If DMs \= 0 for 2 weeks:  
→ Post a direct CTA post: "Reply to this post or DM me if \[specific scenario\] applies to you."

If a specific post gets 3x normal engagement:  
→ Immediately produce 2–3 more posts on the same topic / format. Note it in KNOWLEDGE\_BASE under "successful posts."

| Reels views/video | 500 | 2,000 | 10,000 |  
| Profile visits/week | 50 | 200 | 800 |

\#\#\# Decision Triggers — Instagram  
If Reels underperform (\< 200 views): Check first 3 seconds — is the hook strong? Try trending audio.  
If feed posts get no engagement: Switch to Carousel format. Test different visual styles.  
If followers plateau: Run 3 consecutive Reels in one week. Reels drive follower growth on Instagram.

\#\# YOUTUBE METRICS

Track per video. Review monthly.

| Metric | Target per Video (Month 1–3) | Target per Video (Month 6+) |  
|--------|-----------------------------|-----------------------------|  
| Views (30 days) | 200+ | 1,000+ |  
| Watch time (avg %) | 40%+ | 50%+ |  
| Click-through rate | 3%+ | 4%+ |  
| Subscribers from video | 5+ | 20+ |

\#\#\# Decision Triggers — YouTube  
If avg watch time \< 30%: Video is too long or loses audience early. Shorten or restructure.  
If CTR \< 2%: Thumbnail or title problem. A/B test thumbnails.  
Best performing video: Produce 2 similar videos within 30 days while momentum is high.

\#\# MONTHLY REVIEW CHECKLIST

Every 1st Monday of the month:

Content performance:  
\- \[ \] Which 3 posts got most engagement this month?  
\- \[ \] Which post format worked best (list / story / insight / question)?  
\- \[ \] Which topic drove most DMs or profile visits?  
\- \[ \] What flopped — and why?

Audience growth:  
\- \[ \] LinkedIn follower growth vs. target  
\- \[ \] Instagram follower growth vs. target  
\- \[ \] YouTube subscriber growth vs. target

Lead pipeline:  
\- \[ \] New leads this month vs. last month  
\- \[ \] Any leads moved to Stage 4+ (active opportunity)?  
\- \[ \] Any leads gone cold — what was the reason?

Decisions:  
\- \[ \] Double down on what worked (content type, topic, format)  
\- \[ \] Drop or change what didn't  
\- \[ \] Set 3 specific content experiments for next month

\---

\#\# QUARTERLY STRATEGY REVIEW

Every 3 months, reassess:  
\- Which platform is generating most qualified leads? → Increase investment there  
\- Which target country is showing most engagement? → Create country-specific content  
\- Which ZYT service generates most interest? → Feature it more heavily for 90 days  
\- Is the content-to-lead funnel working? If not, what is the bottleneck?

\---

\#\# VANITY METRICS — DO NOT OPTIMIZE FOR THESE

Likes alone (without comments or DMs): A post can get 50 likes from irrelevant audience and generate zero business.  
Follower count from wrong audience: 5,000 followers who are not CEOs / Procurement Managers \= worse than 500 who are.  
Video views from outside target geography: TikTok virality from irrelevant countries has zero business value.

The only metric that matters for ZYT at this stage:  
Qualified leads (Stage 3+) per month — everything else is in service of this number.
