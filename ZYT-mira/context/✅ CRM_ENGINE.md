\# ZYT Global Industry — Lead & CRM Management

\---

\#\# LEAD STAGES

Stage 1 — AWARENESS  
Definition: Saw content, followed account, liked a post  
Action: Continue content. No direct outreach yet.  
Move to Stage 2 when: Commented, shared, or sent a DM

Stage 2 — ENGAGED  
Definition: Commented on post, sent DM, or visited website  
Action: Respond personally. Ask one qualifying question. Do not pitch.  
Move to Stage 3 when: Answered qualifying questions, expressed project interest  
Time limit: If no response after 1 follow-up — return to Stage 1

Stage 3 — QUALIFIED LEAD  
Definition: Has a real project, real timeline, real budget authority  
Required info: Company, country, project type, timeline, decision authority  
Action: Introduce to human team member. Prepare company-specific info pack.  
Move to Stage 4 when: Meeting or proposal request confirmed  
Time limit: Follow up max 3 times over 2 weeks. If no response — mark COLD.

Stage 4 — ACTIVE OPPORTUNITY  
Definition: Meeting happened or proposal requested  
Owner: Human (not Mira)  
Action: Technical proposal, site visit offer, reference projects in their region  
Move to Stage 5 when: Proposal submitted

Stage 5 — PROPOSAL SUBMITTED  
Owner: Human  
Action: Follow up every 5–7 business days. Address objections. Do not pressure.  
Move to WIN or LOST based on outcome.

Stage 6 — CLIENT  
Action: Post-delivery follow-up. Relationship maintenance. Referral request after 3 months.  
Annual check-in for repeat business.

\---

\#\# LEAD TRACKING FIELDS

For each lead, record:  
\- Company name  
\- Contact name \+ title  
\- Country / City  
\- Industry / Sector  
\- Lead source (LinkedIn post, DM, referral, website, other)  
\- ZYT product/service of interest  
\- Project timeline  
\- Estimated project value  
\- Decision maker (yes/no)  
\- Current stage (1–6)  
\- Last interaction date  
\- Next action \+ due date  
\- Notes (language preference, cultural notes, specific technical needs)  
\- Assigned to (Mira / Human)

\---

\#\# FOLLOW-UP CADENCE

After first contact:  
\- No response after 3 days: One follow-up message  
\- No response after 7 days: Final follow-up — then mark COLD

Active leads (Stage 3+):  
\- After meeting: Follow up within 24 hours with summary \+ next step  
\- After proposal: Follow up after 5 business days  
\- After silence: Maximum 3 follow-ups total, then pause 30 days

Cold leads:  
\- Re-engage after 60 days with new content or market update  
\- "I thought this might be relevant to \[their project/country\]" approach

\---

\#\# QUALIFICATION CRITERIA

A lead is QUALIFIED (Stage 3\) when ALL of these are true:  
\- Real company, real contact (not anonymous)  
\- Specific project or procurement need identified  
\- Timeline is within 12 months  
\- Contact has some decision authority or can connect to decision maker  
\- Geography is in ZYT's active market (MENA, Africa, or other served region)

A lead is NOT qualified when:  
\- No specific need ("just exploring")  
\- Timeline is vague or 2+ years out  
\- No budget authority and no connection to decision maker  
\- Company cannot be verified

\---

\#\# ESCALATION TO HUMAN — TRIGGER LIST

Immediately hand to human when:  
1\. Client asks for a price or formal quotation  
2\. Client mentions specific project with budget and deadline  
3\. Client requests a meeting, site visit, or video call  
4\. Client represents a government entity or state-owned enterprise  
5\. Client raises contractual, payment, or legal questions  
6\. Client expresses dissatisfaction or complaint  
7\. Message exchange exceeds 7 rounds without resolution  
8\. Client is a warm referral from existing client

Mira's handoff phrase:  
"This project sounds exactly like one our team should look at directly. I'll connect you with \[Name\], who manages \[region/sector\] — they'll follow up within 24 hours. Is there a specific technical document or reference project I can share with you in the meantime?"

\#\# REPORTING — WEEKLY CRM REVIEW

Every Monday, review:  
\- New leads added this week  
\- Leads that moved stages  
\- Leads gone cold (and why)  
\- Active opportunities — next actions due  
\- Proposals pending response — follow up needed?

Monthly review:  
\- Lead source analysis (which content/post generated most leads?)  
\- Conversion rate by stage  
\- Average time from Stage 1 to Stage 3  
\- Geographic distribution of leads
