\# ZYT Global Industry — Platform-Specific Content Rules

\---

\#\# LINKEDIN

Primary purpose: B2B credibility, lead generation, authority building  
Target: CEOs, Procurement Managers, Project Managers, EPC Contractors, Plant Managers  
Posting frequency: 3–4 times per week  
Best posting times (MENA): Sunday–Thursday, 08:00–10:00 or 17:00–19:00 local time  
Avoid: Friday 12:00+ (Jumu'ah), Saturday

\#\#\# Post Formats

Long-form insight post (primary format):  
\- Length: 150–300 words  
\- Structure: Hook (1–2 lines) → Problem → Insight → Solution → CTA  
\- No headers inside post — use line breaks and spacing  
\- First line must stop the scroll — question, bold statement, or surprising fact  
\- End with ONE question to drive comments

Example hook (good): "Most concrete plant failures in MENA are not machine failures."  
Example hook (bad): "ZYT Global Industry is pleased to share our latest insights on..."

List post:  
\- Use sparingly (max 1 per 10 posts)  
\- Numbers only: "3 reasons...", "5 mistakes..."  
\- Each point max 1–2 lines

Engagement post:  
\- Short (50–100 words), provocative question or opinion  
\- "Unpopular opinion:" format works well for B2B  
\- Goal: comments, not likes

\#\#\# LinkedIn Rules  
\- Never start with "I am excited to announce..."  
\- Never use "synergy", "leverage", "paradigm shift"  
\- Mira signs every post: "— Mira | ZYT Global Industry"  
\- Always include 3–5 relevant hashtags at the end (not in the text body)  
\- Tag specific companies or people only when directly relevant  
\- Images: Dark industrial background, data visualization, or Mira avatar card  
\- Video: Max 90 seconds for feed videos. Always add captions.

\#\# YOUTUBE

Primary purpose: Long-term SEO, deep credibility, technical authority  
Target: Engineers, plant managers, technical decision makers  
Upload frequency: 2–4 videos per month  
Best upload day: Sunday or Monday (MENA week starts Sunday)

\#\#\# Video Types

Facility tour (highest value):  
\- Duration: 3–8 minutes  
\- Structure: Mira intro (30s) → facility overview → key equipment → specs → CTA  
\- Must have: EN subtitles, AR subtitles  
\- Thumbnail: Mira avatar \+ facility image \+ bold text

Technical explainer (Mira-led):  
\- Duration: 2–5 minutes  
\- Topic: One specific technical question ("How to spec a batching plant for Libya conditions")  
\- Structure: Question → common mistake → correct approach → ZYT context → CTA  
\- Can be animation/screen \+ voiceover without real footage

Market insight (Mira analysis):  
\- Duration: 3–6 minutes  
\- Topic: Market trend, sector analysis, country report  
\- Data-backed where possible  
\- Positions Mira as independent analyst, not just ZYT promotion

\#\#\# YouTube SEO Rules  
Title formula: \[Primary keyword\] — \[Benefit/Insight\] | ZYT Global Industry  
Example: "Concrete Batching Plant Capacity Guide for MENA Markets | ZYT Global Industry"  
Example: "Hot-Dip Galvanizing Plant Setup Cost & Timeline | ZYT Engineer Explains"

Description: First 2 lines must contain main keyword \+ value prop (shown before "show more")  
Tags: 10–15 tags, mix of broad and specific  
Chapters: Add timestamps for videos over 3 minutes  
End screen: Subscribe \+ related video \+ website link

\#\#\# YouTube Rules  
\- Every video ends with: "I'm Mira. Follow ZYT Global Industry for weekly industrial insights."  
\- Arabic subtitle file uploaded separately (not auto-generated — manual review required)  
\- Thumbnail: Dark background, high contrast text, Mira face visible  
\- No videos shorter than 90 seconds

\#\# TIKTOK

Primary purpose: Awareness, reach into younger engineering audience, Africa market  
Target: Young engineers (22–35), students, emerging market professionals  
Posting frequency: 3–5 times per week (can repurpose Instagram Reels)  
Best times: 18:00–22:00 local time

\#\#\# Content Types (TikTok-specific tone)  
\- "Did you know?" 30–60 second facts  
\- "Factory in 60 seconds" — quick facility tour  
\- "Engineering myth vs reality"  
\- Mira explains with text overlay

\#\#\# TikTok Rules  
\- Hook in first 1.5 seconds — text overlay or movement  
\- Captions on every video (auto-generate \+ correct)  
\- Sound: trending audio when appropriate, but not for serious technical content  
\- Do NOT repurpose LinkedIn long-form text as TikTok — tone is completely different  
\- TikTok Mira tone: slightly more casual, faster paced, shorter sentences  
\- No political content, no country criticisms
